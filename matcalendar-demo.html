How MatChalendar works

A single natural-language prompt triggers a cascade of specialist skills, each contributing recommendations — then the Master Planner makes the final calendar decision.

01
💬
Intent Parser

Understands your goals, detects constraints, and determines planning scope — today, this week, or a custom range.

›
02
🧠
Master Planner

Selects relevant skills, collects their recommendations, and resolves any conflicts between domains.

›

Skill System

8 Specialist Skills,
one coherent plan

Each skill is an internal capability, not a separate app. They communicate through the Master Planner — returning recommendations, constraints, scores, and evidence rather than editing the calendar directly.

FIXED 📅
Calendar Skill

Reads fixed events, identifies free slots, and prevents scheduling conflicts.

DATA 🍱
Dining Skill

Recommends UCLA dining options scored by nutrition, timing, and carbon impact.

DEEP 📖
Study Skill

Schedules homework blocks, deep work, and study breaks around deadlines.

BODY 💪
Health Skill

Evaluates meals and activities against health goals, protein needs, and recovery.

STATE ⚡
Energy Skill

Detects low-energy days and softens the schedule — lighter meals, more breaks.

MOVE 🚌
Transportation Skill

Compares walk, bike, bus, and ride-share options by time and carbon cost.

ECO 🌿
Carbon Skill

Tracks weekly carbon budget, detects overages, and recommends compensating actions.

WHY 💡
Explanation Skill

Explains every block — timing, tradeoffs, skills used, and carbon impact.
